# Adhan audio asset

Place your adhan recording here as `adhan.ogg` (also accepts `.mp3`, `.wav`,
`.flac`). It is **git-ignored** and not bundled, since adhan recordings are
generally not freely redistributable — supply your own, or ship a CC0/permissive
recording before distributing.

The applet also checks
`~/.local/share/cosmic-applet-prayer-times/adhan.ogg`.
